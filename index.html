About AstraX

AstraX is the inter-college astronomy themed fest organised annually by the students of Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. Spanning multiple days and including several engaging events, talks & workshops, it is the centre of attraction for students and professors with an inclination towards computational and theoretical astronomy.

Keynote Speakers

Akshat Mohite
A scientist astronaut candidate trainee at project PoSSUM, he was invited by NASA for the 2018 International Space Developmental Conference in the USA. He is currently working on the Project of an American chapter of National Space Society (USA) - Mumbai, India.
Kunal Mooley
A Postdoctoral Researcher at the California Institute of Technology (Caltech), he obtained his M. Sc. Ph. D. in Astrophysics from Caltech in 2015. He is the co-chair of the VLA Sky Survey and co-lead of the JAGWAR program. He specializes in the study of gravitational-wave multi-messenger astronomy, astrophysical jets, and compact star astronomy.
Gabe Gabrielle
Former Director of Engineering for the US Air Force Special Operations Command and Former Contractor/Engineer for NASA at Kennedy Space Center. He is a Member of NASA Speaker Bureau for 11 Years and an International Speaker in 15 countries.
Rajesh Gopakumar
A theoretical physicist and the director of the International Centre for Theoretical Sciences (ICTS-TIFR) in Bangalore, India. He was previously a professor at Harish-Chandra Research Institute in Prayagraj, India. He is known for his work on topological string theory.
Gurtina Besla
Associate Professor in the University of Arizona. She did her Ph.D. in 2011 from Harvard University. She, along with her team, The Besla Group, created detailed simulations of galaxies like the Milky Way and its neighbors and utilized large volume cosmological simulations in order to understand the nature of dark matter and the physics of galaxy evolution.
Nitin Vijay
(Guest Speaker)
Founder and Managing Director of Motion Education Pvt. Ltd, TEDx Speaker and IIT-BHU graduate. He will be giving a talk specially for participants of the School Championship event in AstraX'22.
